The ADR library

15–25

Architecture Decision Records — the most valuable deliverable in the Blueprint

An ADR documents a significant architectural choice: the context, the options considered, the trade-offs, and the rationale for the decision made. When your security team challenges a technology choice, when a regulator asks why a specific data flow was designed as it was, when a new team member needs to understand why the architecture looks the way it does — the ADR library is the answer. Most organisations make these decisions informally and lose the reasoning within months.
